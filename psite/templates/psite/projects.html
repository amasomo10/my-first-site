Holstein Friesian cattle

Project Overview

Nyamasheke Holstein Friesian cattle project aims at setting up an exemplary farm of 8 Friesian Milk Cows with expected milk produce 400 litters daily at the benefit of 6,000 people of Rusizi and Nyamasheke District and Bukavu (Eastern DRCongo region). The operations will be under cooperative COOPERATIVE POUR LA PROMOTION DE L’EVAGE ET D’AGRICULTURE DE NYABITEKIRI (CODEANYA KOTANIRUBWIYUNGE) located in Nyamasheke District in Western Province of Rwanda.


Since its creation in 2010, CODEANYA KOTANIRUBWIYUNGE Cooperative has got a number of achievements including acquisition of 2.5 hectares of land that are used to produce tomatoes with 10 tones of tomatoes of produce per season; rearing 2 cows and 2 hogs; installation of Drip Irrigation System comprised of 2 pumps; and Solar Powered Irrigation System comprised of 36 Solar Panels.


In fact, an article published in March 2019 by The New Times, one of the biggest newspapers in Rwanda, showed CODEANYA as one of the cooperatives that were performing well in terms of managing well the United States African Development Foundation (USADF) grant which enable the them to install the drip irrigation system mentioned above.


CODEANYA is a registered agricultural cooperative by Rwandan government department in charge of national business and investment operations, Rwanda Development Board (RDB). RwandAg confidently believes that CODEANYA achievements and agricultural experience is a a positive indicator that it will deliver its promises. So, we hope that the ongoing good collaboration will lead to the close of Nyamasheke Holstein Friesian cattle investment deal.
