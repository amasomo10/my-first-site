ABOUT RWANDAG

Despite continual Rwanda’s economy growth, agriculture sector faces challenges of accessing finances to improve and expand operations. Rwanda commercial banks reported that only 1.2 percent of their entire loan portfolios went to the agriculture sector in 2018. This makes courageous farmers operate below their potential. RwandAg is here to help Rwandan farmers to access finances from foreign investors.




Opportunity for investors to diversify their investments portfolio

RwandAg connects Rwandan farmers to foreign investors

Growth of agricultural production for farmers and investors returns




MISSION

Connecting Rwandan farmers to agricultural investors for sustainable Rwanda Food Security


MAIN ACTIVITIES

  • Identifying potential farmers and agricultural cooperatives
  • Evaluating prospects’ operations, planned projects, and ensure proper implementation
  • Developing an online platform for farmers with basic services (farm profit calculator) and agricultural updates from major Rwanda agricultural institutions
