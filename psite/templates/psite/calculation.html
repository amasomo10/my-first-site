Farm Profit Calculator

This Farm Profit Calculator is a simple tool we have crafted in order to help you estimate the seasonal short term (planting to harvest) Net Profit of your agricultural activities. Fill in your seasonal numbers and click on "Calculate" button. Plug in zero in a place that does not apply to you.


All amount should be the equivalent value in RWF - Rwandan Franc


  • Sales is the lump sum of all income from your seasonal agricultural activities.

  • COGS: Cost of goods sold is the total direct cost of required materials used to produce the goods sold. Cost of feed in case of cattle and pesticides in case of crops can be included here.

  • Expenses includes rent, salaries, transport cost, communication, and veterinary costs in case of cattle and other expenses that are related to work efficiency.

  • DA: Depreciation and Amortization are the non-cash charges that are crucial for calculating net profit of your agricultural activities. Depreciation is the declining in asset value of the course of its useful life. Click here for calculation and details. Amortization is the same thing except that is used for an intangible asset over a specific period of time.

  • Interest is here considered as the cost charged on borrowed money. If you do not have bank loan or did not borrow money, this does not apply to you.

  • Tax: is a compulsory contribution to state revenue. It changes from year to year. Check out more information about Personal Income Tax, District Revenues, and VAT - Value Added Tax services if you do agricultural commercial activities.
